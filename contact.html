About Me

Hi! I’m Kathryn Cruz, and I am an undergraduate student majoring in computer science at the University of Notre Dame and computing and applied mathematics at Saint Mary’s College.

I initially made this site for my Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program (SIP) students after providing them with a chaotic spreadsheet with many links and resources. However, I hope this site becomes a resource for those outside of my SIP students. As a full time student, I've been updating these recourses as much as possible during my free time, but please reach out to me if you have found more programs or resources to be added. Also, feel free to reach out to just connect or ask questions!
